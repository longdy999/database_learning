## Common Mistakes to Avoid

1. **Cramming before the exam** — review steadily, not all at once
2. **Ignoring weak areas** — spend extra time on topics you struggle with
3. **Writing messy SQL under pressure** — always use proper formatting
4. **Forgetting to explain WHY** — clients need reasons, not just answers
5. **Skipping documentation** — your portfolio must be self-explanatory
6. **Not practicing speaking** — reading SQL is different from explaining it
7. **Perfectionism on portfolio** — done is better than perfect
